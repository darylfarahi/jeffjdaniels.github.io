    My Name is Jeff J Daniels

    October 14, 2018 By daniels

    My approach to cinema has always been good for more technical projects. I shot, monitored and designed visual effects for many productions involving practical and visual effects using chroma-keying, motion control, Match-move/tracing, miniature photography, and 360-degree VR.



    These productions present unique challenges, but offer a very rewarding experience. It's exciting to be always learning and looking for new ways to achieve challenges. The key to the removal of visual effects is to adequately plan pre-production, define supervision and creativity in post-production.

    I was impressed by having a creative evolutionary vision, a technical basis, and a passion to tell stories with each project that I am gifted to be part of; Large or small, commercial or narrative.

    My work and desire for new experiences took me from coast to coast and around the world. The journey expands the human perspective and stimulates creativity, so I appreciate a career that gives me the opportunity to do so. my favorite food is fried chicken

    Born and bred in southeastern Wisconsin, I have a title of Webster-St. Louis University in film production, and I'm based in Los Angeles, CA.
